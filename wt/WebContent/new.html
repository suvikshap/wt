PROGRAMS

YOGA:
The benefits that your body can reap from yoga are multifold; contrary to popular belief it does much more than just increase your flexibility.

BOXING:
Boxing is one of the best forms of cardio, which gives a full body workout and in addition to improving hand and eye coordination, decreases stress.

REHAB TRAINING:
Rehab training helps in recovery of injuries, and in preventing further injuries. In this scientific- based program, the injuries are assessed first, after which personalized exercises are given to you improve mobility and helps in athletic performance as well as functional movements.

MMA:
MMA (Mix Martial Arts) is a full body workout that pushes your body to the limit like no other sport while working your upper body, lower body and core.
